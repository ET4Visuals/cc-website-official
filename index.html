About Us

Capture Connecticut is a community-based platform that highlights and supports photographers all across the state. It was founded by Eddie, a high school photographer who started shooting at 15 and quickly realized how hard it was for young creatives to get noticed. At just 16, he took a risk and decided to build something different — a page where local photographers could get real exposure without needing to be famous or have thousands of followers. That’s how Capture Connecticut was born.

We’re all about giving photographers the spotlight they deserve. Whether you’re shooting sports, portraits, concerts, landscapes, or creative edits — if you’ve got talent and passion, we want to show your work to the world.

No matter what you need — sports, senior pics, events, or just cool shots — we’re your go-to place for coverage in CT. At Capture Connecticut, we got your back. Whether you're looking to get featured or need a photographer for your shoot, we’re here to help make it happen.

What We Do

• Repost and feature local photographers across Connecticut
• Help you grow your page and get your name out there
• Build a creative community where people support each other
• Connect creators so you can collab, meet new people, and grow together
• Keep it 100% free — no fees, no paywalls, no BS
